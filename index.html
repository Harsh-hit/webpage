WHO ARE WE?

Communicando is the address for all things literary. We are an eclectic concoction of language, literature, poetry, exploration and expression at CBIT. Over the past 15 years, Communicando has not only grown immensely to be one of the largest clubs in CBIT but has also established itself as the pioneer for creativity and exuberance through a series of events and segments held throughout the year. We at Communicando embrace the unique ingenuity of everybody by hosting events that are bound not by language but by love and enthusiasm for wit, words, and repartee. The club has always promoted an inclusive and holistic space for growth where everybody feels welcome. From readers to authors to poets to designers to dramatics and ramblers, we have a place for everybody.

EVENTS

BOOK MARK

We at Communicando aim to re-kindle the joys of reading in this age of Instagram reels and deteriorating attention spans, through our monthly book meet - BookMark. BookMark is a gathering of diverse taste profiles and opinions, which makes it a wonderful experience for both avid and new readers. BookMark is an open and informal assemblage that welcomes both readers and non-readers to explore and expand their literary horizons. BookMark provides a safe space to share, grow, engage in meaningful conversation, and let out your inner literary geek, whilst engaging in fun activities that let you get to know your fellow book lovers.

CRESCENTIA

Crescentia stands as one of the most anticipated yearly events, professionally and skillfully coordinated by Communicando. The distinguished gathering unites the entire college community for a day filled with inspiring discussions, captivating exchanges, and unbridled enjoyment. Crescentia graces us with “Enchante”, a speaker event where a notable icon or eminent figure is our exclusive guest speaker. Apart from Enchante, Crescentia hosts a day of intellectually stimulating games and literary events, which give you a chance to step into the dragon's den, negotiate, create, and entertain your way to victory. Crescentia aspires to be more than just an event; it is a transformative experience to learn from the best , one who has challenged their boundaries and emerged a leader. Whether you're competing in spirited challenges, participating in collaborative team activities, or simply enjoying the camaraderie, "Crescentia" promises an array of experiences that will leave you with unforgettable memories.
